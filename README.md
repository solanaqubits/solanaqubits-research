# Solana Qubits Research

Markdown research materials from Solana Qubits.

This repository is intended for transparent, source-linked research notes about Solana validators, staking, ecosystem infrastructure, quantum technologies, post-quantum cryptography, and long-term technology literacy.

The stake pool research tracks public validator scoring dashboards, delegation dashboards, active application or interest links, and source-linked program updates.

The quantum computing research snapshot tracks public scientific publications, standards documents, cloud/HPC context, vendor-stated roadmaps, and industry/adoption reports with cautious source attribution.

## Contents

- `articles/solana-stake-pool-delegation-landscape.md` — practical snapshot of Solana stake pool delegation programs for validators.
- `articles/quantum-computing-in-2026.md` — cautious public research snapshot on quantum computing progress in 2026, including error correction, logical qubits, hardware approaches, benchmarks, cloud/HPC integration, adoption barriers, and post-quantum cryptography migration context.
- `sources/solana-stake-pool-links.md` — source links used for the stake pool delegation landscape research note, including public validator dashboards, delegation dashboards, application/interest links, and program updates.
- `sources/quantum-computing-in-2026-links.md` — source links used for the quantum computing 2026 research snapshot, grouped by standards/PQC, peer-reviewed research, cloud/ecosystem, industry/adoption reports, and vendor/company materials.
