This source list groups evidence by type. Standards and peer-reviewed papers carry different evidentiary weight than vendor materials and industry reports. Vendor roadmaps are vendor-stated signals, not independent validation. IQM/The Quantum Insider is treated as an industry/adoption report, not peer-reviewed technical proof.

## Standards / PQC

- NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography Project  
  https://csrc.nist.gov/projects/post-quantum-cryptography  
  Use for NIST PQC standardization context and continuing algorithm work.

  https://www.nist.gov/news-events/news/2024/08/nist-releases-first-3-finalized-post-quantum-encryption-standards  
  Use for public NIST framing of FIPS 203, FIPS 204, FIPS 205, and migration urgency.

- IETF PQUIP working group  
  https://datatracker.ietf.org/group/pquip/about/
